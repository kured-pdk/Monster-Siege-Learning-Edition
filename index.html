How to Play

1. Move with WASD / Arrow Keys on PC, or use the touch controller/click targets on mobile devices.

2. Your weapon fires automatically towards the closest monster, or you can click anywhere to aim.

3. Answer math questions to earn Scrap. Just 3 consecutive correct answers triggers a massive MONSTER WIPE! In multiplayer, both host and client can input solutions dynamically.

4. Build & upgrade defenses using scrap. To build, purchase a turret type and tap a clear slot on the map.

5. If you fail to answer within the 20-second continuous timer, or submit 5 wrong answers, a random defense turret is lost.

6. Survive 20 waves to claim victory!

7. Math Monsters (❓) can only be destroyed by answering a math question correctly – bullets don't hurt them!

💡 Hint: Use the 💡 Hint button (50 scrap) to reveal the correct answer! In Kid Mode, you get +10s extra time for 30 scrap.

🎮 Tutorial: Click the 🎓 Tutorial button on the home screen for a guided walkthrough.

✨ Cheats: Press ` or ↑↑↓↓←→←→BA to open the cheat console (3 uses per game).

🏆 Achievements: Your progress is saved automatically! Unlock badges and earn bonus scrap.
